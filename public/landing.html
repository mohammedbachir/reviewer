Install Reviewer

Follow these steps to add Reviewer to your Chrome browser.

1

Download the extension

Download the extension from GitHub and extract it to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Type chrome://extensions in your address bar and press Enter.

3

Enable Developer mode

Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner.

4

Load the extension

Click Load unpacked and select the extracted extension folder.

5

You're ready!

Open your Google Business Reviews page and you'll see the Give it Answer button on each review.
